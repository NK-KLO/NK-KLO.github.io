Project: KLOx

KLYx is a modular Quality Management System designed to support scalable, compliant, and commercialization-ready GMP operations.

Why It Exists

Many legacy QMS platforms are rigid, fragmented, or not built for modern biotech scaling. KLYx is being built to demonstrate how modular architecture, risk-based workflows, and clean system design can support growth from clinical to commercial manufacturing.

Current Progress

  • Backend architecture initialized
  • Modular structure defined
  • Initial database models drafted
  • Authentication flow prototyped
